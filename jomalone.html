Jo Malone // Gifting App

A gifting application to allow users to purchase gifts outside of the ecommerce site.

My Role: I worked with a UX Designer who supplied wireframes. Collaboratively, we worked together to ensure the best possible user experience. I worked under the direction of an Art Director to create the full design of the application. The challenge was referencing the already existing brand of Jo Malone, while moving away from the ecommerce site. We were in turn, able to create a new, fresh environment which felt more personalized for a great application experience.

Tools Used: Photoshop & InVision
